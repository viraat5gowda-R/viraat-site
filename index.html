Foundations

Trained inside the machine.

Before directing my own films, I learned how worlds are built at scale — inside one of the largest productions Indian cinema has ever mounted. Five years on KGF Chapter 2 placed me close to the parts most first-time directors never see until their own first budget.

How a set of hundreds is run. How action and VFX are scheduled in the same breath. How discipline holds a frame when the canvas is the size of a city. How a director's clarity — or absence of it — ripples through every department by 6am.

It shaped how I work. Ambition without control is noise. The bigger the vision, the quieter and more exact the execution must be.

Working under director Prashanth Neel on KGF Chapter 2 taught me that scale is a discipline, not a resource.
